Zenith

This project involved the creation of a "Choose Your Own Adventure"-style game, inspired by many text based adventure games in the golden age of video games and by Black Mirror: Bandersnatch. The game was created entirely by myself including all of the code; written in Java using the Processing IDE; text prompts and storyboard; in-game 8-bit graphics; original theme song; etc. Gameplay involves being presented a prompt of the player's current position as well as two options to choose that traverse through the world. The objective of the game is to to reach the top of Mount Zenith and defeat the final boss seated at the summit; along the way various side questions and boss fights will require precise navigation, skill, and wits.

The link below directs to Game Jolt where the game is currently being released for FREE to play for all!

Choose your destiny as you try to climb to the top of Mount Zenith. This text based adventure game will require your wits, navigation, and skill, so only the best can make it to the top. Do you have what it takes? #arcade #retro #puzzle #textadventure #adventure

This project was completed in just 10 days during the winter of 2021. Future updates to the game may include more paths featuring new boss fights, puzzles, locations to explore, and in-game sound effects & scores.
