About Me

I am an aspiring robotics engineer passionate about joining the aerospace industry and continuing to apply my project experiences to professional roles.

I am a passionate learner by nature and love to be thrown head-first into challenging projects and new material. From my diverse work experiences and educational interests, I excel at applying extraordinary solutions to extraordinary challenges from both an analytical and hands-on perspective.

I've developed tools over the years to solve such problems from developing new software across a variety of programming languages, conducting computational fluid dynamics simulations for aerospace designs, performing finite element analyses for critical failure studies, and many more. My hands-on background has also given me ample experience in working with and repairing mechanical systems, rapid prototyping designs, and collecting experimental data in a range of environments.

I strive to aim high and am excited for the next project that will come my way!
